ABOUT MS Global (Main Stream Global Education)

The Best Solutions For Better Tomorrow

Main Stream Global Education ( MS Global ) is a business consulting company based in Kathmandu, Nepal, supporting local and international organizations to expand their business in different geographic locations around the world. Nepal is the primary destination for us and currently, we are associated with different international organizations for establishing their business activities in Nepal. Primarily we are focusing on educational projects, human resources consulting, technological transformation, the agriculture sector, and Health care.

Our Vision

The company is founded with the vision of exploring business opportunities for companies without boundaries and bringing Opportunities to everyone.

Our Objective

The major objective of the company is to provide global business opportunities to everyone and MS global always believes in collaboration, not competition.

We create the B2B business opportunities in education, human resource management,

technology transformation, agriculture, and Health Care
